OUR SERVICES

Guided tours with experienced guides who will provide visitors with a safe and educational experience.

Rental services for hiking equipment, such as backpacks, tents, camping gear, hiking boots, and the like.

Accommodation

Transport to the starting points of tours or provide transport services between different mountain locations.

Special tours for photography lovers so that you can capture the beautiful landscapes and nature of Montenegro.

ONE DAY TOUR

8 hours
13.3 km
950 m

Embark on a remarkable journey to the breathtaking Mount Hajla, reaching an impressive altitude of 2004 meters, nestled in the heart of Montenegro. Our carefully curated one-day tour invites you to immerse yourself in the mesmerizing landscapes and pristine wilderness as you ascend to the summit, guided by our team of seasoned experts well-versed in the region's rich history and natural wonders. Revel in the panoramic views that unfold at each step, capturing the essence of Montenegro's unparalleled beauty.

After an exhilarating climb, treat yourself to a well-deserved break and savor the flavors of traditional Bosnian cuisine, lovingly prepared to tantalize your taste buds. Indulge in an authentic culinary experience, sampling a variety of local delicacies that reflect the vibrant culinary heritage of the Bosnian culture. Amidst the serene surroundings and warm hospitality, take a moment to relish the fusion of flavors and aromas that encapsulate the essence of the region.
